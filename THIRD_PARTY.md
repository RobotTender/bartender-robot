## Doosan Robotics ROS2 Packages

Repository:
https://github.com/DoosanRobotics/doosan-robot2

License:
BSD 3-Clause License

Description:
These packages provide ROS2 drivers, interfaces, and motion control utilities for Doosan collaborative robots.

Usage in this project:
The packages are used for controlling the Doosan E0509 robot arm and executing robot motion commands within the bartender robot system.
